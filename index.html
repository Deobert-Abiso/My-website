Phonk

Phonk is a genre of music that was created in the early 1990s by DJ Screw. It is a style of hip hop that is characterized by slow, heavily-mutated beats and sampled vocals. Phonk is often associated with the Houston hip hop scene, as it was created and popularized by DJ Screw and his crew, the Screwed Up Click.

Instrumental

An instrumental is a recording without any vocals, although it might include some inarticulate vocals, such as shouted backup vocals in a Big Band setting. Through semantic widening, a broader sense of the word song may refer to instrumentals. The music is primarily or exclusively produced using musical instruments.
